Many rebuilds and changes

One of the curiosities of Valdivia is that it has suffered several catastrophes and has been rebuilt several times since its foundation. Only in the 20th century did it have two destructive events. In 1909 a fire destroyed the center of the city. During the reconstruction, its physiognomy changed completely when a new type of layout, more modern and functional, was planned. On May 22, 1960, in the afternoon, an earthquake measuring 9.6 on the Richter scale shook southern Chile and mainly affected the city of Valdivia. Until today, this was the strongest earthquake in history.
